Your Meal Plan

Everyone has to eat the 5 food groups in order to maintain a healthy balanced lifestyle. We can help you plan this on a weekly basis so you don't have to worry about your calorie intake or meals for the rest of the week. You can focus on your studies and still eat right!

Here are some examples of the five food groups:
Carbohydrates: pasta, rice, oats, potatoes, noodles, yams, bananas, bread, cereals, barley, corn.
Protein: meat, fish, eggs, vegetable protein, nuts, beans, peas, lentils, tofu, whey.
Dairy: milk, yogurt, cheese (cottage cheese, cream cheese), butter.
Fruit: apples, oranges, pears, plums, grapes, raisins, prunes, apricots, watermelon.
Vegetables: lettuce, cabbage, broccoli, spinach, celery, asparagus, eggplant, brussel sprouts, carrots, onions, califlower, kale, greenbeans, radish.
Tips for portions (modify based on body type):
1/2 plate = fruits and veggies, vary veggies and focus on whole fruits
1/4 plate = proteins
1/4 plate = carbs, half of total grains should be whole grains
